What is Video Editing?

Video Communication Technology supports a student driven learning environment which allows students to explore media driven methods of digital technology. The primary goal is to develop lifelong learners that embrace the connections across the curriculum. Students will acquire and demonstrate a solid knowledge of creativity and technical process as well as maintain a level produce professional quality work that will enable them to be successful at the next level.
